Project: Steal Stuff

Go fast and steal the cassette player.

Project: Steal Stuff is a fast paced stealth game where you dodge enemies and collect loot all while in a randomly generated level. You play as a faceless thief who wants to steal old world technology like cd players and notboys' and get the most coveted loot of all: the cassette player. While on your escapade you must not only get the loot but do so quickly and sneakily because as the guards learn of your nefarious deeds they will come in greater numbers to stop you in your tracks! Steal and try to get the high score with expert movement and stealth mechanics!
